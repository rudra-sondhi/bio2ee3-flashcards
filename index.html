How to use these

  • Midterm vs Final. Each deck has a toggle up top: 🎓 Final = everything; 📝 Midterm = MCQ / no-typing (hides the fill-in & table cards) for quick multiple-choice review.
  • Rate honestly. Both decks use Anki-style grading — Again / Hard / Good / Easy. Cards you find hard come back sooner; a card is "mastered" at level 4.
  • Filter to what you need. Use the chips at the top: ★ Priority for the prof-flagged testable items, 📝 Past Quiz to drill the real quiz questions, or pick a single subchapter.
  • Type your answers. Fill-in-the-blank and table cards have real input boxes — type, hit Check, and get marked instantly.
  • Label the diagrams. On labelling cards the labels are covered — name each number, then Reveal.
  • Keyboard: Space flip · 1–4 grade · Enter check · → skip · T stats.
  • On your phone: open this page, then Share → Add to Home Screen for an app icon.
